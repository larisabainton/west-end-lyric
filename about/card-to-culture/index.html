Card to Culture

West End Lyric is pleased to participate in the Card to Culture program, a collaboration between Mass Cultural Council and the Department of Transitional Assistance, the Massachusetts Health Connector, and the Women, Infants, & Children (WIC) Nutrition Program by extending discounts to EBT, WIC, and Connector Care cardholders.

  • For admission-based performances, admission is free with an eligible EBT card.
  • For admission-based performances, admission is free with an eligible WIC card.
  • For admission-based performances, admission is $5 (unlimited) with a Connector Care card.
